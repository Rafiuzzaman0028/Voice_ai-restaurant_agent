# Guide: How to Retrieve Your ElevenLabs API Key and Voice ID

Hello! To bring your AI Voice Agent to life with the most realistic and expressive text-to-speech, we use **ElevenLabs**. We need you to provide your specific API Key and your preferred AI Voice ID to link your account to the restaurant order system.

Please follow these quick steps:

## Step 1: Create an Account & Upgrade
1. Go to [ElevenLabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io/) and create an account.
2. **Important:** Because we are using streaming text-to-speech for real-time phone calls, you must upgrade to at least a **Starter** or **Creator** paid plan. Free plans do not have the required concurrency or latency limits needed for a seamless phone conversation.

## Step 2: Get Your Secret API Key
1. Once logged in, look at the bottom-left corner of the dashboard and click on your **Profile Icon**.
2. Click on **Profile + API Key**.
3. Under the "API Key" section, click the **eye icon** to reveal your key, and click the **copy icon** to copy it.
4. Paste this key securely in an email or message to the developer.

## Step 3: Select Your Preferred Voice ID
1. In the left-hand navigation menu, click on **Voices**.
2. Listen to the different voices available and find one that perfectly fits your restaurant's brand (e.g., *Rachel, Adam, Callum*).
3. Once you find the voice you want to use, click the **View/Details** button for that specific voice.
4. Look for the **Voice ID** (this is usually a string of random characters, for example: `pNInz6obpgDQGcFmaJcg`). 
5. Copy this Voice ID string.

## What to send the developer:
Please send the following two pieces of information:
- **ElevenLabs API Key:** `sk_xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx`
- **Preferred Voice ID:** `xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx`

Once provided, your customized voice will answer all incoming restaurant phone calls!
